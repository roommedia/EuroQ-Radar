While the tech world is hyper-focused on GPU clusters, Europe is quietly assembling an incredibly robust, full-stack **Quantum-AI Ecosystem** that remains heavily undervalued. 

**EuroQ-Radar** is an open-source structured data matrix tracking everything from Top-level JRC policy and Trapped-ion foundries to aerospace/defense integration. 

---

## 🗺️ The 7-Layer Golden Closed-Loop

EuroQ-Radar categorizes the industry into seven interconnected strategic pillars. Click to filter the network:

1. **Top-level Policy & Standards:** Defining European digital sovereignty.
2. **National HPC + QC Infrastructure:** The computing backbone fusing supercomputers with early QPUs.
3. **Hardcore QPU Foundry:** Superconducting & Trapped-Ion hardware physical layer.
4. **Algorithmic & Compilation Layer:** Translating quantum physics into functional software design.
5. **System Integration & Consulting:** Driving enterprise "Quantum Readiness".
6. **Industrial End-Users:** Aerospace, Defense, and Finance leading the early deployment.
7. **Legal, Compliance & Capital:** Guardrails, Senate hearings, and Deep-Tech VC lobbying.

## 🚀 How to Contribute

EuroQ-Radar is a community-driven radar. Found a new quantum hardware spin-off in Munich? Or a bank implementing QML in Milan? 

We welcome PRs! Please ensure your data follows our 6-attribute database schema.

1. Fork the Project
2. Create your Feature Branch (`git checkout -b feature/AmendedPioneer`)
3. Commit your Changes (`git commit -m 'Add PioneerX to Hardware Layer'`)
4. Push to the Branch (`git push origin feature/AmendedPioneer`)
5. Open a Pull Request
